I shot this many years ago, early to mid 90's.  It's a nice time exposure with my Nikon FM-2N camera, a totally manual 35mm camera.  I used a stopwatch and a time release cable to time the exposure just right. I bracket the exposures to get the right photo. I used a flashlight to light up the sign.
