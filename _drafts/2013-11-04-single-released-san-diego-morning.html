I've just released my first ever song.

It's a big deal to me. I've come a long way from owning a couple of companies to also making music.

I started the song while on a business trip for one of my companies in San Diego. I was up high in a hotel overlooking San Diego Bay and I would spend my morning creative time creating beautiful music from my balcony.

Hope you enjoy the chillout song.
