I’m a writer focused on helping people unplug from noise and rediscover what actually matters. After a seizure reshaped my life and made me hypersensitive to technology, I rebuilt everything around meaning, creativity, and slower, more intentional living. Through my essays, books, and art, I share simple ways to live well in a wired world — not through perfection, but through presence.

→ Read my full story

What I Write About

Meaning

Life feels better when you’re clear on what truly matters. I explore the small, honest choices that lead to a fulfilling life — not the ones that look good on a vision board.

Technology Boundaries

We live in a wired world, and most people are drowning in it without noticing. I write about healthier ways to relate to tech so your mind, energy, and creativity actually return.

Creativity

Art, writing, and self-expression aren’t hobbies — they’re ways of remembering yourself. I share how to create from the inside out, without hustling your soul away.

Fulfilling Work

You don’t need extreme ambition or extreme minimalism. You need meaningful work that supports a life you actually want to live. I write about real-world, non-hypeful ways to get there.

Slow Living

Not the Instagram version. The grounded, practical version where you stop racing long enough to enjoy the life you’ve already built.
