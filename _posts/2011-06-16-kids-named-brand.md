Robin Williams and his daughter, who was named after Zelda the video game, appear in an ad for the new Zelda game "The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time 3D".

You know you have a great brand name when people name their kids after it. Heck, someone named their kid Facebook recently. That's cruel. But Zelda? That's cool.

Have a great product and be consistent about it and you too can have a name people want to name their kids after, even if it is a horrible name like Facebook.
