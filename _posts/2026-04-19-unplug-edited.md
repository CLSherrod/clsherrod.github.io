I finally finished editing _[Unplug](/courses/unplug/)_. In the video below, I share that update and also walk through three simple ways to reduce EMF exposure at work. The main point is this: don’t guess. **Measure your actual environment**, make a few practical changes, and start where you spend the most time.

This kind of testing changed how I set up my own environment.

## Start With These 3 Changes
1. **Get an EMF meter and test the places where you actually sit and work.**  
    Most people assume they know where exposure is coming from, but a meter often shows surprises.
2. **Create a charging area away from your body.**  
    Don’t keep devices charging right next to you on a desk or beside your bed if you can help it.
3. **Small setup changes can matter more than people think.**  
    Moving a router, changing where you charge devices, or adjusting where you sit can make a meaningful difference over time.

I learned the hard way that guessing is not the same thing as knowing.

If you want a deeper step-by-step approach, _[Unplug](/courses/unplug/)_ goes further into how I think about reducing exposure in everyday life.

Start by measuring what’s around you. It’s usually more useful than worrying in the abstract.
