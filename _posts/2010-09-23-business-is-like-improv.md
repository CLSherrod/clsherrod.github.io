I recently took an improv class. I loved it so much I signed up for the full course, which starts in a week. What I noticed while doing improv is that it's a lot like doing business.

You have to be okay with improvising your way through it. Your core skill will help, but many things are going to be new. Just be your authentic self. And don't care what others think about you. Just go out there and do it. You'll get better. But you'll struggle if you think too much about it.
