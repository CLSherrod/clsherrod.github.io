I write fiction as another way of exploring truth.

Where my essays speak directly, fiction lets me work through story, memory, atmosphere, and the deeper emotional currents that shape a life.

## Upcoming Book: _When Ocho Remembered_

_When Ocho Remembered_ is an upcoming novel about memory, identity, and the truths that wait beneath the surface.

Some things are forgotten.  
Some things are hidden.  
Some things return when the time is right.

More details coming soon.

## Why Fiction

I write nonfiction to say things plainly.  
I write fiction to say them differently.

My fiction is reflective, layered, and more interested in meaning than spectacle.
