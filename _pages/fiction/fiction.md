_Stories about memory, mystery, place, and the strange edges of life._

I’m best known for writing essays about living a fulfilling life, but fiction gives me another way to explore what matters. Sometimes a story can say something truth cannot reach directly.

They take place in familiar worlds and lean toward the speculative, the supernatural, or the surreal. All of them are part of the same body of work.
