*Stories about memory, mystery, place, and the strange edges of life.*  
  
I’m best known for writing essays about living a fulfilling life, but fiction gives me another way to explore what matters. Sometimes a story can say something truth cannot reach directly.  
  
For now, I’m focusing on short fiction. These stories may be quiet or unsettling, grounded or strange, but they all come from the same place: attention, curiosity, and a deep interest in what it means to be human.  
  
Some take place in familiar worlds. Some lean toward the speculative, the supernatural, or the surreal. All of them are part of the same body of work.  
  
## What You’ll Find Here  
  
- Short stories

## Current Direction  
  
Right now, I’m writing fiction one piece at a time.  
  
I’m not interested in rushing out a brand or pretending I have a large catalog before it exists. I’d rather publish strong work, let it accumulate naturally, and build this section with care.  
  
That means this page will grow slowly.  
  
I’m fine with that.

## Why Fiction?  
  
Because some things are better revealed through story.  
  
Fiction lets me follow memory, place, emotion, and the unseen logic of a life. It lets me ask different questions. It lets me make room for wonder, dread, beauty, and change.  
  
It also lets me write in a way that feels alive.
