## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I have to get rid of Wi-Fi?

No. The course explains several ways to reduce unnecessary wireless exposure without demanding that you eliminate Wi-Fi completely.

### Is this an anti-technology course?

No. Technology can be enormously useful. Unplug is about using it intentionally rather than allowing it to operate everywhere, all the time, without thought.

### Do I need to purchase an EMF meter?

No. A meter may be useful for some people, but you can make many practical changes without purchasing one.

### Will I need to replace my devices?

Probably not. Many of the first changes involve distance, placement, settings, timing, and turning devices off when they are not needed.

### Does the course require expensive products?

No. The course emphasizes simple behavioral and environmental changes. It also helps you evaluate whether a tool or product is genuinely useful before spending money on it.

### Can I take the course slowly?

Yes. The course is entirely self-paced. The included Personal Unplug Plan will help you choose a manageable starting point.

### Is this course medical treatment?

No. Unplug is an educational course. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition.
